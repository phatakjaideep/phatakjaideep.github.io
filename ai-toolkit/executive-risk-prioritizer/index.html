Methodology & Security

What the Executive Priority Score represents. A 0–100 deterministic, weighted composite: Business Impact 25%, Urgency 20% (includes a capped deadline accelerant), Strategic Importance 15%, Dependency Impact 15%, Mitigation Confidence Gap 15%, Ownership & Decision Gap 10%. The same inputs always produce the same score.

Deadline accelerant. Overdue: +15 points to normalized urgency. Due ≤2 days: +12. Due ≤7 days: +8. Due ≤30 days: +3. "No date applicable" and "Ongoing monitoring" apply no accelerant. "Date not established" on a risk requiring a decision, or with business impact 5, adds a governance gap penalty instead (see below).

Ownership & decision gap. No risk owner: +45 (of 100 on this sub-scale). Decision required with no confirmed decision owner: +40. Decision required with a confirmed owner: +15. "Date not established" on a decision-required or executive-critical risk: +20 additional governance-gap points.

Probability. Probability is displayed for conventional risk context and the heat map, but it is not directly weighted in the Executive Priority Score.

Closed risks. Risks with status "Closed" are excluded from all scoring, counts, cards, charts, and exports by default. You may opt in to including them via "Include closed risks in analysis."

Exposure areas. Areas such as Launch, SOP, Safety, or Compliance are explicit, user-selected classifications — not inferred automatically from a Business Impact score of 5. CSV imports without an Exposure Areas column receive a cautious, clearly labelled inference from category/title/description text.

Suggested Governance Level & escalation. Escalation is derived from a combination of decision requirement, missing ownership, deadline criticality, mitigation weakness, dependency footprint, strategic/regulatory exposure, and score — never from score alone. Escalation Required and Executive Visibility can also be set explicitly by the risk submitter.

Workstream Exposure Index. 40% normalized average score, 25% normalized peak score, 15% executive-critical concentration, 10% leadership-decision concentration, 10% weak-mitigation concentration.

Advisory only. Human judgment, regulatory review, safety assessment, and formal governance approval remain required. This tool does not replace them.

Local intelligence assistant. The embedded assistant is not a live large language model, not generative AI, and not machine learning. It uses predefined keyword and intent matching against your currently loaded, analysed dataset.

Security controls. Strict Content Security Policy; no fetch, XMLHttpRequest, WebSocket, cookies, or browser storage of any kind; all CSV and form input treated as plain text and rendered via safe DOM APIs; spreadsheet formula neutralization on CSV export; no external fonts, scripts, or stylesheets; no analytics or tracking.
